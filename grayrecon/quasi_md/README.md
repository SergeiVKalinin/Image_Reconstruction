# Quasi-MD models

Grayscale reconstruction methods based on artificial particle dynamics and inference force fields.

This module will include:

- particle and reconstruction state definitions;
- rendering of particle configurations into grayscale images;
- configurable effective force fields;
- stochastic and overdamped dynamics;
- particle insertion, trapping, and capture;
- ensemble reconstruction and uncertainty estimation.

The dynamics represent an inference process rather than physical molecular dynamics. Force-field parameters, particle states, trajectories, and energy contributions will remain directly accessible to the user.
