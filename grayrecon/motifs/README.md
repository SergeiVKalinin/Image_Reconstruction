# Motif models

Grayscale reconstruction methods based on local structural motifs and their spatial relationships.

This module will include:

- motif definition and extraction;
- motif covariance and rigidity;
- matching of complete and incomplete neighborhoods;
- motif adjacency and compatibility rules;
- propagation of motifs into missing regions;
- reconstruction uncertainty from alternative motif assignments.

The lower-level motif interface will expose motif geometry, covariance, matching tolerances, adjacency rules, and inferred motif assignments.
